D.S. Nelson

Independent Systems Theorist

D.S. Nelson researches constrained informational systems, lawful emergence under structural constraint, and epistemic failure dynamics across physical and informational domains.

Preprint published January 20, 2026 via Zenodo. This paper develops a formal account of how constraint structures stabilize lawful behavior in complex systems, modeling emergence as a function of structured informational compression.
